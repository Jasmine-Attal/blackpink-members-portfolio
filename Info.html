Information on BLACKPINK(블랙핑크)

BLACKPINK is a well known K-pop Girl Group by YG Entertainments. It had kept the Title of the group (Black stands for Power and Pink stands for Girl) Hence it means "Girl Power".

It has Four members

  • Lisa
  • Jennie
  • Jisoo
  • Rose

The band debuted on August 8th, 2016 under YG Entertainment. On October 23, 2018, BLACKPINK has officially signed with the U.S. label Interscope Records.

The Blackpink Fans are Officially Called Blinks.
